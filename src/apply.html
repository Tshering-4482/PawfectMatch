DISCLAIMER:
Thank you very much for your interest. Please remember that while we review every application carefully, submitting one does not "claim" an animal. Once we receive your application, we will make an appointment for you to come to meet the pet you are interested in or to visit our shelter to work with an adoption counselor to find a great match. We do not hold animals for adoption. Adoptions are on a first-come, first-serve basis to qualified homes.
